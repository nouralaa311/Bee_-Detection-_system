Use-case recommendations
Real-time detection / edge deployment
Use YOLOv8n (best.pt) — 6.6× smaller, ~3–4× faster inference. Fits on Raspberry Pi, mobile, or any latency-critical pipeline. mAP@0.5 is identical to the larger model.
High accuracy / tighter localization
Use YOLO11m (best11m.pt) — superior mAP@0.5:0.95 (+2.88 pp), higher recall (+3.93 pp), and lower losses across all three loss heads. Better bounding box precision at stricter IoU thresholds.
Critical caveat — different datasets
The two models were trained on different datasets (bee_dataset vs Queen-Bee-4). This comparison reflects model capability per dataset, not a direct architecture comparison. Retrain both on the same split for a fair head-to-head evaluation.
